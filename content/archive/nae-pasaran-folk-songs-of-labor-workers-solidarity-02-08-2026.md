This Sunday, remember who brought you the weekend! Join dj gopher for Orange County Special—WXYC’s weekly segment on all things folk, country, gospel, and bluegrass—for Nae Pasaran: Folk Songs of Labor, Workers & Solidarity.

From the Scottish mines to the South African coal trains, from Appalachian textile factories to Mexican-Filipino farmworkers, we’ll explore the sounds of working folk all around the world. Join Sunday 02/08 12-2pm.
