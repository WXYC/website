WXYC is excited to partner with this year's Big Pop Show, taking place Friday, March 20th, through Monday, March 23rd across the Triangle at The Pinhook and Duke Coffeehouse in Durham, and The Arts Center and Cats Cradle in Carrboro. 

If you're looking to support local and visiting up-and-coming artists, you can check out this year's lineup, find additional info, and purchase tickets at bigpopshow\.neocities.org.
